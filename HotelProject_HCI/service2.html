Dynasty

Infomation

Dynasty is an elegant, spacious and beautifully designed restaurant decorated in a contemporary Chinese style that has been serving some of the finest authentic Cantonese cuisine in Ho Chi Minh City for two decades.

A longstanding favorite among Vietnamese and international guests alike, Dynasty offers a fantastic à la carte menu that includes a great dim sum selection along with traditional Chinese dishes including Roasted Duck, Suckling Pig and Braised Abalone in Oyster Sauce.

Every weekend the Yum Cha buffet offers a variety of dim sum from early morning. The spread includes more than 40 kinds of dim sum served with various buns, noodles and rice.

With an interior as pleasing to the eye as the food is to the palate, Dynasty features four semi-private and three private dining rooms with illuminated artefacts and original lantern chandeliers that create just the right atmosphere for family gatherings and celebrations, as well as business lunches and dinners.

Open Hours

  • Yum Cha buffet: 9 a.m. to 3 p.m. (weekends only)
  • Quick Lunch: 11:30 a.m. to 3 p.m. (weekdays only)
  • Lunch: 11:30 a.m. to 3 p.m.
  • Dinner: 6 to 10 p.m.
