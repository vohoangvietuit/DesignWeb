New World Saigon Hotel offers 25 One-Bedroom Suites at 69.5 square meters, two Director’s Suites at 91 square meters and one Presidential Suite at 181 square meters. With impressive views of the stunning city skyline, the spacious, Suites are provided with luxurious Elemis toiletries.
